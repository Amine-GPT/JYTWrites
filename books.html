Books & Essays

"The Understudies"

Novel · In progress

Tibor Fellowship 2024 · Tin House Summer Workshop 2023

A reluctant theater understudy on an off-Broadway production of Hedda Gabler begins to suspect the lead is sabotaging herself on purpose — and that she's the only one who can tell.

"Somewhere Between Places"

Essay Collection · In progress

A book of essays on the in-between architecture of American life — train cars, hotel lobbies, dental waiting rooms, and the specific loneliness of being in a place designed to be left.

"All Your Friends Are Pretending"

Novel · In development

A dryly funny coming-of-middle-age novel about four women who realize, at their ten-year college reunion, that none of them have been telling the truth since graduation.
